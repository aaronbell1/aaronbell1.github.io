About

Aaron Bell, Web Developer based in Leicester, UK.

As a developer the landscape of technology is always changing, and I relish the challenge of staying up-to-date with and learning new things in order to stay relevant.

Above all, after being in the industry for a few years now, I have come to learn that my main passion lies in writing clean, reusable and maintainable code using the many useful tools I have at my fingertips.

I also promote working in the most adaptable way possible and, having taken on project management responsibilities in my career, I generally look towards Agile principles in order to achieve project goals in the most flexible way possible.

Things I enjoy

  • Writing code as cleanly as possible with tests to ensure maintainability
  • Demystifying the world of development
  • Automation of tedious tasks
  • Moving systems away from pen and paper
  • Encouraging flexibility both in projects and in the workplace
  • Gaining knowledge and experience to help influence future decisions
  • Constant improvement of my own workflow
